THE MISSION AND VISION OF DONAR2NEEDER

DONAR2NEEDER IS A NON-PROFIT ORGANISATION.
CONNECTING DONAR TO NEEDER.

ABOUT DONAR2NEEDER

MISSION

Increase awareness: The website's mission is to increase awareness about the importance of blood donation and its impact on saving lives.

Connect donors with recipients: The website aim to connect donors with people in need of blood transfusions, making it easier for recipients to find donors and receive the blood they need.

Ensure the safety of donors and recipients: The website is prioritize the safety of donors and recipients by following established guidelines for blood donation

Overall, the mission of our website is to make it easier for people to donate blood and to ensure that those in need of blood transfusions can find the help they need.

VISION

Ensure access to safe and adequate blood supply: The website's vision is to ensure that everyone who needs blood can access a safe and adequate supply of blood.

Encourage a culture of voluntary blood donation: The website is promoting the importance of voluntary blood donation and strive to make it a part of people's regular routine.

Foster a sense of community: The website is foster a sense of community among blood donors and recipients, creating a supportive network of people who are passionate about helping others.

Overall, the mission of a our website is to make it easier for people to donate blood and to ensure that those in need of blood transfusions can find the help they need.
